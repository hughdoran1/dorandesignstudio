Product Ontology Schema

Every product portfolio is a connected system. The number of configurations and use cases quickly exceeds what traditional, linear user documentation can support. Content becomes outdated, fragmented, and costly to maintain across languages and markets.

Doran Design Studio creates a Product Ontology Schema to map your products and their relationships, for example:

Product → available in → Region Product → compatible with → Product

We then model the relationships between products, markets, and the content required to communicate them across all formats, touchpoints, and audiences—transforming complex product data into structured, actionable documentation. This includes modeling what new content is required for a product launch, what must be updated, and where every asset is located—so delivery happens in the minimum number of process steps.

Purpose Built Database and Automation

Structuring content in this way enables a purpose-built database and automation layer. Content is created once and reused across documents, interfaces, and channels, with updates applied automatically wherever it appears.

This approach supports dynamic product documentation, reduces manual rework, and ensures consistency across regions, languages, and formats. The result is scalable, continuously up-to-date product communication aligned to real product data and real-world use cases.
